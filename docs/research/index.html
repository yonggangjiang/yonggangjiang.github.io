Hardness of bi-criteria approximating shortcut

Problem “$(\alpha,\beta)$-bi-criteria $(s,d)$ shortcut” ask for distinguish two type of directed raphs

  • adding $s$ shortcuts will reduce the diameter to at most $d$,
  • adding $\alpha s$ shortcuts cannot reduce the diameter to less than $\beta d$.

Current Progrosses

Under PGC conjecture, $(n^\epsilon,n^\epsilon)$-bi-criteria is not possible for polynomial algorithm for

  • $d=n^c$ for any $0<c<1$ and $s=n^{\epsilon}$ for small constant $\epsilon$.
  • $s=n^{1+\epsilon}$ and $d=n^\epsilon$ for small constant $\epsilon$.

Warning: only hand-writing.

Future Goals

Writing the lower bounds.

Do something for upper bound.

Large Vertex Connectivity

Currect Progresses

For unweighted undirected simple graph vertex connectivity we have

  • $O(n^{1.5})$ communication complexity

  • $\sqrt{T_{matching}n}$ congest

  • $O(n^{1.5})$ passes $\sqrt{P_{matching}n^2}$ streaming

For weighted undirected edge partition simple graph vertex connectivity/ unweighted undirected edge partition multi graph vertex connectivity, we have $\Omega(n^2)$ lower bound.

Future Goals

  • Improve the communication complexity upper bound (and further improve in congest and streaming).
  • Explore the communication complexity of vertex partion weighted case, whether we can break $n^2$ or not.
